The Cupping Room, Canberra, ACT

All innuendo that the name inspires aside, The Cupping Room is about a serious as it gets for coffee aficionados. Created by the guys from Ona Coffee, they know their farmers, their beans and how to extract the right amount of nuance into every brewing technique and style of coffee. From cold drip filter over ice, to pour overs and expertly pulled espressos, The Cupping Room is not only talented in the bean department, but serves up a substantial cafe menu, sourced with the same respect for produce as the beans enjoy. Must try: Feel like iced tea but don’t want to miss out on the coffee action? Try the iced cascara, tea made from the cherry of the coffee tree. The Cupping Room, 1/1-13 University Ave, Canberra ACT, (02) 6257 6412
