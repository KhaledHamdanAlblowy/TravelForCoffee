Australia?? Why

Australia's take coffee very seriously. Although Italian migrants introduced espresso coffee in the post-World War II period, it wasn't until the 1990s that Aussies' obsession began. A style of espresso coffee created in Australia, the 'flat white', has even started turning up in the cafes of New York and London.
