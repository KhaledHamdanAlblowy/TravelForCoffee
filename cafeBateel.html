Cafe Bateel

Café Bateel is one of the most famous café’s in Saudi and has many branches around Saudi. The café offers different styles of Arabic coffee: “qahwa shamaliyah” which means northern style coffee and its known for its dark colour and strong flavour, “qahwa alqassim” which means Al-Qassim Region coffee and it’s known for its light sandy colour and cardamom flavour, and many other styles of coffee
